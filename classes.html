Muay Thai boxing is a full body contact sport originally from Thailand. It has been around for centuries and uses stand-up striking (no ground fighting) and clinching techniques. It is also known as "the art of eight limbs" and encompasses the use of fist, elbows, knees, and shins. Muay Thai is recognized as an international professional sport and governed by various overseas entities.

Jiu-jitsu is another combat sport that originated from Brazil in the early 1900s. It is a sport known for taking down opponents and engaging them in ground fighting. Emphasis is placed on position and grappling techniques such as submission holds (i.e. chokes and joint locks). Jiu-jitsu has many registered federations and tournament organizers.

Kickboxing is a high intensity cardio workout that incorporates moves from boxing, martial arts, and aerobics. Throughout the one hour class, you will be throwing jabs, crosses, hooks, and uppercuts as well as round house kicks, front kicks and side kicks. Kickboxing gets your heart racing, burns lots of calories, and is a great stress reliever!
