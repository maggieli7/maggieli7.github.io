Bruce Harrison

Bruce Harrison is the owner and instructor of Blended Martial Arts. The studio was founded in 2014 and is currently located in NYC.

Bruce has been a long time practitioner of several martial arts form for 26 years. He continues to train in muay thai boxing, jiu jitsu, jeet kune do, and kali. Bruce has participated and won a few grappling tournaments most notably...

His style of teaching is comprehensive in that it combines some forms of martial arts rather than one specific form. He organizes his teachings based on a rotating curriculum that is not tied to the belt ranking system. Students in various phases of training learn the same material together before moving on to the next level. This system of learning promotes team building, discipline, and self-respect.

Class usually opens with 20 minutes of body stretches, drills (i.e. jump rope, shadow box, heavy bag), and sprints. He then reviews lessons from prior class before moving on to new material. Bruce not only advises, but explains the technique of each move deliberately so that it reinforces the student's learning and helps to improve their form. The class ends with 20 minutes of repetition sit-ups, push-ups, and cool down.

Brue strives to help individuals bring out the best in themselves. His philosophy approach is achieve a strong mindset and body achieve success in life.
